About Me

DevCraft Learner!

Hello there, I'm Shubhrant Tripathi, a passionate explorer currently pursuing my B.Tech in CSE with Artificial Intelligence at PIET-Jaipur. I thrive on the thrill of tackling real-world challenges and embarking on a perpetual quest for knowledge. With a heart that beats for coding, I dive into the realms of HTML, CSS, JS, Python, and C. I also wield the power of frameworks like Bootstrap and JS to craft digital marvels.

Graphic design is my canvas, where Adobe Software is my brush. I weave UI/UX enchantments, breathe life into digital art, and sketch stories through captivating visuals. From banners to t-shirt designs, stickers to flyers, I create designs that resonate and leave an indelible mark.

My journey has seen the birth of intriguing IoT projects. Home automation by relay modules and Nodemcu. Visualize GPS systems powered by Esp32 and Neo6m GPS modules. These are just the opening notes of an ongoing symphony of innovation.

In the world of internships, I donned the hat of a Python Software Developer at Zeetron Networks, shaping the digital landscape one code at a time. My campus ambassadorial flair extends to platforms like Coding Ninja's and Careers 360, where I thrive in fostering innovative collaboration.

Driven by boundless curiosity, I'm on a perpetual quest to uncover the secrets of technology's tapestry. Feel free to reach out at ishubtripathi@gmail.com, and let's unravel the possibilities together!

I'm also a proud member of the lead organizing team of a hackathon conducted by my college, where innovation and creativity converge.
